# ICC Corporate Cases Database

## Relevant Precedents for Corporate Complicity

### Katanga (IT-01-45/1-T, 2014)
- First ICC case involving corporate personnel in war crimes
- Established precedent for individual liability within corporate structures
- Corporate support for militia groups constitutes aiding and abetting

### Kenyan Cases (ICC-01/09-01/11) 
- Corporate complicity through telecommunications and surveillance systems
- Precedent for technology companies facilitating mass surveillance
- Establishes liability for corporate systems enabling crimes

### Corporate Complicity in Myanmar/Burma (2018-2023)
- BAE Systems, Total, etc. providing infrastructure enabling genocide
- ICTY precedents applied to corporate liability
- Due diligence obligations for corporations operating in conflict zones

### European Court of Human Rights - Mass Surveillance Cases
- Big Brother Watch v. UK (2021) - Mass surveillance violates Article 8
- Zakharov v. Russia (2015) - Surveillance oversight requirements
- Silver v. UK (1983) - Balance between security and privacy
