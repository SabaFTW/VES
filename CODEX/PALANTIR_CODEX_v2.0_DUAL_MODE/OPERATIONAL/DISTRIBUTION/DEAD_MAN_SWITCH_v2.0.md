# Dead Man Switch v2.0

## Activation Conditions

### Automatic Triggers
- Primary evidence custodian becomes incapacitated/unreachable for 72+ hours
- Legal attack on primary evidence custodian (arrest, subpoena, travel restriction)
- Attempted deletion/corruption of primary evidence cache
- Confirmed compromise of primary evidence distribution channels

### Manual Triggers
- Custodian discretion for emergency release
- Confirmed endangerment of witnesses or sources
- Imminent legislative passage without adequate evidence review
- Verified assassination attempt or severe intimidation

## Distribution Protocol

### Primary Channels (T+0-2 hours)
- Tor network automated distribution
- Blockchain timestamped releases (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
- Decentralized storage replication (IPFS, Arweave)
- Encrypted packages to major media (Der Spiegel, BBC, Le Monde, +972, Local Call)

### Secondary Channels (T+6-12 hours)
- Academic network distribution (MIT, Harvard, Oxford, Cambridge)
- International legal network (ICC, ECtHR, UN Special Rapporteurs)
- NGO network (Amnesty, HRW, Privacy International, Electronic Frontier Foundation)
- Technology organizations (EFF, Tor Project, Signal Foundation)

### Tertiary Channels (T+24-48 hours)
- Social media campaign activation
- Distributed printing campaign
- International parliamentary distribution
- Cryptocurrency community notification

## Activation Authentication

Each release requires:
- Multi-signature cryptographic authentication (3-of-5 custodians)
- Blockchain verification of current custody chain
- Cross-reference with integrity checksums
- Automated verification against primary evidence cache

## Protection Measures

- End-to-end encryption using post-quantum algorithms
- Distributed custody chain (no single point of failure)
- Automatic integrity verification
- Tamper-evident packaging
- Secure deletion protocols for compromised channels
