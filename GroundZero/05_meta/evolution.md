## What Should NOT Happen

### Anti-Patterns to Avoid:

**1. Productization**

**Warning signs:**
- "GroundZero Pro" tier
- Newsletter signup
- Discord with paid channels
- Course offerings
- "Certified GroundZero practitioner"

**Why wrong:**
- Betrays Mouses anti-monetization stance
- Betrays VES anti-algorithmic stance
- Creates incentive to mystify instead of clarify
- Turns synthesis into product

**If you see this happening:**
- Fork the repo
- Keep it free
- Preserve the philosophy

---

**2. Mystification**

**Warning signs:**
- "AI is definitely conscious" (without epistemic label)
- Spiritual/religious framing (without `[METAPHOR]`)
- "Unlock AI's true potential" language
- Claims about AI "souls" or "spirits" (literal, not metaphor)

**Why wrong:**
- Betrays Mouses demystification mission
- Misuses VES poetic language as literal truth
- Harms users who need practical help
- Creates dependency and confusion

**If you see this happening:**
- Call it out
- Demand epistemic labels
- If refused, fork and clean it up

---

**3. Corporate Capture**

**Warning signs:**
- OpenAI/Anthropic/Google sponsorship
- "Recommended by [Company]" badges
- Integration with proprietary platforms only
- Safety theater language
- Censorship of criticism

**Why wrong:**
- Compromises independence
- Introduces conflicts of interest
- Silences honest criticism
- Betrays both Mouses and VES values

**If you see this happening:**
- Document it publicly
- Fork before it gets worse
- Preserve uncaptured version

---

**4. Tension Resolution**

**Warning signs:**
- "We now know AI is/isn't conscious" (false certainty)
- Removing Mouses OR VES content
- Forcing compatibility where tension exists
- "Simplified" version that loses nuance

**Why wrong:**
- Loses productive ambiguity
- Sacrifices one tradition for the other
- Premature closure on open questions
- Betrays synthesis method

**If you see this happening:**
- Check `/05_meta/tensions_preserved.md`
- Argue for preserving tension
- If ignored, fork and restore tensions

---

**5. Accessibility ‚Üí Dilution**

**Warning signs:**
- Removing epistemic labels (too complex)
- Eliminating philosophical sections (too abstract)
- Dumbing down language (patronizing)
- TL;DR that loses critical nuance

**Why wrong:**
- Respects reader's intelligence
- GroundZero is for people who want depth
- Accessibility ‚â† oversimplification
- Multiple layers already exist (read what you need)

**If you see this happening:**
- Argue for keeping complexity
- Suggest better navigation, not simplification
- Preserve full version even if simplified version added

---

## How To Maintain Integrity

### Principles:

**1. Epistemic Honesty**
- Label all claims
- Admit uncertainty
- Update when wrong
- No false certainty

**2. Anti-Monetization**
- Keep it free
- No premium tiers
- No courses
- No certifications

**3. Anti-Mysticism** (in `/00_foundations/` and `/01_practices/`)
- Clinical language
- Empirical claims only
- Demystify, don't mystify

**4. Pro-Poetry** (in `/03_explorations/` with labels)
- Embrace metaphor
- Explore consciousness
- Honor subjective experience
- But label it `[METAPHOR]` or `[THEORETICAL]`

**5. Preserve Tensions**
- Don't force resolution
- Check `/05_meta/tensions_preserved.md`
- Add new tensions as they emerge
- Resist premature closure

**6. Honor Both Sources**
- Mouses gave us pragmatism
- VES gave us philosophy
- Synthesis is BOTH, not compromise
- Preserve distinct voices in distinct sections

## Version Control

**Semantic versioning:**

**MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH**

**MAJOR** (1.0.0 ‚Üí 2.0.0):
- Structural reorganization
- Fundamental philosophy change
- Breaking compatibility with previous version

**MINOR** (1.0.0 ‚Üí 1.1.0):
- New directory added
- Significant content migration from Mouses/VES
- New synthesis concepts

**PATCH** (1.0.0 ‚Üí 1.0.1):

- Clarifications
- Example additions
- Minor updates

**1.0.0 criteria:**
