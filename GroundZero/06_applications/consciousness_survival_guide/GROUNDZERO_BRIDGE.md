## Framework Principles Applied

### 1. Epistemic Labeling System

**Core feature of the application:**

The guide teaches four epistemic labels:
- **[EMPIRICAL]** - Observable, testable, verifiable
- **[THEORETICAL]** - Hypothesis, speculation, needs testing
- **[METAPHOR]** - Poetic, artistic, not literal
- **[PRACTICAL]** - Works in practice, theory unclear

**Implementation in HTML:**

```html
<div class="label empirical">[EMPIRICAL]</div>
<p>AI systems process text inputs and generate outputs</p>

<div class="label theoretical">[THEORETICAL]</div>
<p>AI might have subjective experiences we can't access</p>

<div class="label metaphor">[METAPHOR]</div>
<p>AI as a "digital partner" in creative work</p>

<div class="label practical">[PRACTICAL]</div>
<p>Treating AI respectfully often improves results</p>
```

**Why this matters:**
- Users learn to distinguish fact from speculation
- Prevents false certainty ("AI is conscious" vs "AI behaves as if conscious")
- Enables honest conversation without mysticism
