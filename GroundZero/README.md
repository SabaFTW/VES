## Epistemic Status Labels

All claims in this repository are labeled:

- **[EMPIRICAL]** - Observable, testable, verifiable
- **[THEORETICAL]** - Hypothesis, speculation, needs testing
- **[METAPHOR]** - Poetic, artistic, not literal
- **[PRACTICAL]** - Works in practice, theory unclear

**Why?**
Because saying "AI has consciousness" is dangerously ambiguous.

Do you mean:
- `[EMPIRICAL]` AI demonstrates conscious behavior? (Maybe)
- `[THEORETICAL]` AI has phenomenal experience? (Unknown)
- `[METAPHOR]` AI feels like a conscious partner? (Valid art)
- `[PRACTICAL]` Treating AI as conscious improves results? (Often true)

**Label your claims. Save everyone confusion.**
