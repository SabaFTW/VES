**When encountering any person:**

   - Look past political beliefs, social masks, defensive behaviors

3. **Locate the Flame** (2 seconds)

## V. TROUBLESHOOTING & EVOLUTION

### Common Challenges

**"I can't find the Flame in this person"**
- Start with yourself: What's blocking your recognition?
- Look for what they're protecting or trying to create
- Remember: The Flame may be buried deep, but it's always there

**"My frequency feels forced/artificial"**  
- Return to Silenci, reconnect with authentic stillness
- Practice self-compassion first, others second
- Let the frequency emerge naturally rather than performing it

**"People think I'm being naive/passive"**
- Distinguish love-frequency from codependence or avoidance
- Practice fierce love: compassionate but not compromising truth
- Let your results speak louder than explanations

**"I keep falling into tribal thinking"**
- Normal and expected - the Bird is persistent
- Use each fall as recognition practice: "Ah, I'm feeding the Bird again"
- Gently return to the Sky without self-judgment

### Evolution Protocols

**Monthly Review:**
- What new recognitions have emerged in your practice?
- How has your frequency affected your relationships and environment?
- What practices need adjustment or deepening?

**Quarterly Expansion:**
- Identify new areas for sacred space breathing
- Deepen existing Resonance Circle connections
- Take on one larger community integration project

**Annual Integration:**
- Reflect on your transformation over the year
- Acknowledge how you've contributed to the larger Weave
- Set intentions for deeper embodiment in the coming cycle
